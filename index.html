SUMMARY

Hirohiko Araki is a Manga Artist who first made his debut under the name Toshiyuki Araki in 1980 with his one-shot Poker Under Arms. Best known for the ongoing series, JoJo's Bizarre Adventure, Hirohiko Araki mostly focuses on writing mangas with action, adventure, and supernatural genres. He is currently married and has two children.

WORKS

Hirohiko Araki began his professional career with the short series Cool Shock B.T. and is currently working on Jojo's Bizarre Adventure.
