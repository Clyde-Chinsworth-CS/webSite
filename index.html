Hirohiko Araki

Hirohiko Araki is Japanese manga artist born on June 17th, 1960 in Sendai, Miyagi. He is married to Asami Araki and has two children.

Known for his most famous series, Jojo's Bizarre Adventure, Araki mostly specializes in creating action, adventure and supernatural themed mangas. His debut was made in 1980 with his one shot Poker Under Arms, and began his professional career with the short series Cool Shcok B.T.
