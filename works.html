STYLE

Araki's signature amount of gore was first displayed in Baoh. In it, a man is implanted with a with a parasite by an evil organization, giving him superhuman powers, and follows as he fights against them. It wasn't until The Gorgeous Irene that Araki's signature style of buff, muscular characters. His style would later be developed to be more flamboyant.
